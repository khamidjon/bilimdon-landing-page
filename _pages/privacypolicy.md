# Privacy Policy
Thank you for using Bilimdon! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

<br>

## 1. Information We Collect
Bilimdon is designed to help you test your knowledge through quizzes in Uzbek. We may collect the following types of information:

- Player Profile: Nickname, avatar, and quiz scores you create within the app.
- Quiz Data: Your quiz answers, scores, streaks, and leaderboard rankings.
- Usage Data: Anonymous data such as session length, app crashes, or feature usage, to improve performance and user experience.

We do not collect any personally identifiable information unless explicitly stated.

<br>

## 2. How We Use Your Information
We use the information you provide to:

- Track your quiz scores and progress across categories.
- Display your ranking on the weekly leaderboard.
- Maintain your daily streak and challenge history.
- Improve app performance and user experience.

Your quiz data may be synced to our servers for leaderboard functionality.

<br>

## 3. Third-Party Services
Bilimdon may use trusted third-party tools for analytics, crash reporting, or ad delivery. These services may collect limited technical data, such as device type or operating system version. No personal data beyond what is necessary is shared.

We may update this section if new services are added in future versions.

<br>

## 4. Data Security
We take data security seriously. All data transmitted between the app and our servers is securely handled using industry-standard encryption.

You are responsible for protecting your device and any backups you make.

<br>

## 5. Your Rights
You have full control over your data. You can:

- View or edit your player profile.
- Delete your data at any time.
- Uninstall the app to remove all local data.

## 6. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We'll notify you of any major changes through the app or app store listing.
