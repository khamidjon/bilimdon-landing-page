# Bilimdon

**O'zbek tilidagi viktorina ilovasi** — an Uzbek-language quiz app that makes learning fun.

## What is Bilimdon?

Bilimdon is a mobile trivia app where users test their knowledge across various topics in Uzbek. Play quick quizzes, compete with others on the leaderboard, and build daily streaks to stay motivated.

## Key Features

- **5 Quiz Categories:** Tarix (History), Fan va Texnologiya (Science & Tech), Geografiya (Geography), Adabiyot (Literature), Sport
- **250+ Questions** across all categories with 3 difficulty levels
- **Smart Question System:** The app remembers which questions you've already mastered and shows you fresh ones each time
- **Weekly Leaderboard:** Compete with other players for the top spot every week
- **Daily Streaks:** Play every day to build your streak and stay consistent
- **Daily Challenge:** A new category challenge every day
- **Player Profiles:** Customizable nickname and avatar
- **Timed Gameplay:** Answer before time runs out for maximum points
- **Score Multiplier:** Watch a short ad to double your quiz score

## How It Works

1. Open the app — no sign-up needed, you're in instantly
2. Pick a category or play the daily challenge
3. Answer 5 timed multiple-choice questions (A, B, C, D)
4. See your results and optionally double your score
5. Climb the weekly leaderboard and keep your streak alive

## Target Audience

- Uzbek-speaking students and learners
- Trivia and quiz enthusiasts
- Anyone who wants to test general knowledge in Uzbek
